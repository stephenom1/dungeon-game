How to Play

In this game, you play as a dungeon crawler, you must survive waves of ghouls.

Controls

Use the "WASD" keys to move your character around the screen. Press "J" to attack with your sword. Press "SPACE" to roll, while rolling you may dodge attacks and become invincible for a short time.

Scoring

Your score is how many enemies you defeat and is displayed below the game.

Health

Your character has a health bar that will decrease if you take damage from enemies. It will regenerate over time while you are out of danger.

Game Over

If your character's health bar reaches zero, the game is over.
